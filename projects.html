My Projects

This page highlights the projects I worked out throughout the semester. This website, is the accumulation of everything I have learned in COMP 2511, Web Development.


This page is part of the ePortfolio, which will be the focus as I discuss and illustrate the technical and design knowledge I obtained.

Technical

The technical decisions I made is for ease of readability and utility. For example, wrapping everything into divs in order for my code to be readable when I come back to work on it. For example, this webpage is based off of my assignment 2, as it fulfills the form factor that I need.

Some of the challenges I faced while designing the website is the responsiveness and using grids. However, it only required time and a little bit of tweaking to obtain the desired layout.

Design

The design of the website is intentional, personally, I don't like flashy and strong colours that pop out. I prefer the muted and subtle colours that are easy on the eyes. Additionally, it applies from the Gestalt principles in order to make the website appealing to viewers.

The image above is the colour palette I used for this website. I had to tinker with the palette as I wanted colours that are subtle and are easy on the eyes.

Challenges

Like I mentioned earlier in the technical section, some of the challenges I've experienced is formatting the grid template for the website. As I had to play around with grid columns to place each section where they belong depending on the size of the screen.

Once I formatted the website to the layout I desired, everything else quickly fell into place. Another small hurdle I experienced was coding the responsiveness of the website, coding the mobile hamburger menu when to show up on the appropriate screens. However, as I read and worked through the problem, it eventually fell into place as well

What Could Be Improved
